SYSTEM PARAMETERS (TUNED)
================================================================

Base K-Factor: 40
Starting Rating: 1400
Rating Range: 600 - 2200

K-Factor by Experience:
  0-19 matches:   K=50 (New players - fast convergence)
  20-49 matches:  K=45 (Developing players)
  50-149 matches: K=40 (Established players)
  150+ matches:   K=32 (Veterans - stable ratings)

Minimum for Established Rating: 30 matches
  Below 30 matches = "Provisional" rating

Margin Scoring (Poules): Enabled
  Formula: Actual Score = 0.5 + (Your Score - Their Score) / 10
  5-0 = 1.0, 5-1 = 0.9, 5-2 = 0.8, 5-3 = 0.7, 5-4 = 0.6

DE Bracket Weights (multiplier on K):
  Finals (L1-2):      5.0×
  Semifinals (L1-4):  4.0×
  Quarters (L1-8):    3.0×
  Bronze (L3-4):      4.0×
  L1-16:              2.5×
  L1-32:              2.0×
  Mid brackets:       1.8-2.5×
  Lower brackets:     1.0-1.2×

Field Size Scaling: sqrt(field_size / 20)
  Adjusts for tournament size (observed range: 11-29 fencers)

Placement Bonuses:
  1st place:  +15 rating
  2nd place:  +10 rating
  3rd/4th:    +5 rating
